Unlike Wizden, GS14 chemistry is focused around reagents with massive recipe trees and complex conditions to induce mixing. This means that the disparity between a chud like you who has this guide and someone who doesn't is extremely high, as they're going to have to naviagate through 30 different menus to find the recipe for sulfuric acid while you have to do none of that.

Core Meds

  • Salicyic Acid - Brute. Acts like a combination of the Advanced Brutes.
  • Oxandrolone - Burn.
  • Artiplates - Bloodloss, Blood Level.
  • Tirimol Dex+ - Airloss.
  • Pentenic Acid - Toxin, Purges Bloodstream.
  • Atropine - Stabiliser.
